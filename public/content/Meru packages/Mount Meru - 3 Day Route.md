# Mount Meru Routes

The 3 Day Mount Meru Route

| Route summary |  |
| :---- | :---- |
| Length: | 3 days |
| Type: | Huts |
| Difficulty: | Moderate to Demanding |

# Day 1: Momella Gate to Miriakamba Hut

| Trekking time | 4-6 hours |
| :---- | :---- |
| Climatic zone | Mountain Forest |
| Altitude gained | 1,000 metres / 3,280 feet |

After breakfast, you will be picked up from your hotel in Arusha and driven to Momella Gate in Arusha National Park. The drive takes less than an hour. After registration with Tanzania National Parks (TANAPA), your trek begins accompanied by an armed ranger, as you are hiking through a national park with abundant wildlife.

The first day is similar to a walking safari. You can expect to see giraffes, zebras, antelope, buffalo, and potentially elephants near the trail. The path passes through open grassland before entering lush montane forest. You will cross the Ngarenanyuki River and pass a beautiful waterfall at the base of Mount Meru. Lunch is enjoyed at the famous Fig Tree Arch.

After lunch, the trek continues through less dense forest where you may spot black and white colobus monkeys and a huge variety of birds. By mid-afternoon, you will have your first closer views of the towering cliffs and the ash cone. Miriakamba Hut is reached in time to enjoy the last of the afternoon sun and pleasant views over the surrounding plains towards Kilimanjaro.

Overnight at Miriakamba Hut (2,500m). Meals: Lunch and Dinner.

# Day 2: Miriakamba Hut to Saddle Hut

| Trekking time | 3-4 hours |
| :---- | :---- |
| Climatic zone | Moorland |
| Altitude gained | 1,050 metres / 3,445 feet |

After breakfast, the path becomes steeper as you ascend along the ridge of the saddle. The landscape becomes more varied and interesting as the rainforest gives way to moorland and bush vegetation. The trail has stunning views of Mount Meru Crater, the ash cone, and Little Meru peak along the way.

Keep your eyes peeled for wildlife including buffalo and the black and white colobus monkey. Your trek ends at Saddle Hut (3,550m), where you will spend the night. After resting at camp, you have the option of hiking Little Meru (3,820m) with your guide for acclimatization. This one-hour trek offers spectacular views and is highly recommended.

You will be served an early dinner so you can receive ample rest before your midnight summit attempt. Get to sleep by 1900.

Overnight at Saddle Hut (3,550m). Meals: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner.

# Day 3: Saddle Hut to Socialist Peak to Momella Gate

| Trekking time | 13-14 hours |
| :---- | :---- |
| Climatic zone | Alpine Desert / Summit |
| Altitude gained | 1,016 metres / 3,333 feet |

We start very early in the morning, around midnight, for the summit attempt. From Saddle Hut, a steep ascent in short loops leads to the gap between Little and Big Mount Meru, and from there we continue to Rhino Point. Passing through rock and scree, we reach the main peak.

The final part of the hike follows a dramatically narrow ridge line between the steep inner cliffs and the sloping outer wall of the crater. Socialist Peak (4,566m) is reached in time for a spectacular sunrise. On a clear day, you will have a fantastic view of Mount Kilimanjaro floating above the clouds, as well as panoramic views of Arusha National Park.

After celebrating at the summit, we descend back to Saddle Hut for a short rest and brunch. We then continue down to Miriakamba Hut and finally to Momella Gate. Your driver will be waiting to transfer you back to your hotel in Arusha or Moshi.

Meals: Breakfast and Lunch.
