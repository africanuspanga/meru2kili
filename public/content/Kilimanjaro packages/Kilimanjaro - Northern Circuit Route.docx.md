# Kilimanjaro Mountain Routes

## The Northern Circuit Route

| Route summary   |                      |
| :-------------- | :------------------- |
| **Length:**     | 8–9 days             |
| **Type:**       | Camping              |
| **Difficulty:** | Moderate             |
| **Best for:**   | Highest success rate |

---

# **Day 1: Londorosi Gate to Mti Mkubwa Camp**

| Trekking time       | 3–4 hours   |
| :------------------ | :---------- |
| **Climatic zone**   | Rainforest  |
| **Altitude gained** | ~800 metres |

Same starting point as Lemosho route. The trek begins through dense rainforest leading to Mti Mkubwa Camp.

---

# **Day 2: Mti Mkubwa to Shira 1 Camp**

| Trekking time     | 5–6 hours |
| :---------------- | :-------- |
| **Climatic zone** | Moorland  |

Gradual ascent into moorland and onto the Shira Plateau with expanding views.

---

# **Day 3: Shira 1 to Shira 2 Camp**

| Trekking time     | 3–4 hours |
| :---------------- | :-------- |
| **Climatic zone** | Moorland  |

Short acclimatization trek across the plateau.

---

# **Day 4: Shira 2 to Lava Tower to Moir Camp**

| Trekking time     | 6–7 hours   |
| :---------------- | :---------- |
| **Climatic zone** | Semi-desert |

Climbers ascend toward Lava Tower and then continue to Moir Camp, beginning the northern circuit.

---

# **Day 5: Moir Camp to Buffalo Camp**

| Trekking time     | 5–7 hours     |
| :---------------- | :------------ |
| **Climatic zone** | Alpine desert |

The route moves into remote northern slopes with fewer climbers and wide panoramic views.

---

# **Day 6: Buffalo Camp to Third Cave Camp**

| Trekking time     | 5–7 hours     |
| :---------------- | :------------ |
| **Climatic zone** | Alpine desert |

A steady traverse across the northern side of the mountain.

---

# **Day 7: Third Cave to School Hut**

| Trekking time     | 4–5 hours     |
| :---------------- | :------------ |
| **Climatic zone** | Alpine desert |

Climbers reach base camp (School Hut) and prepare for the summit attempt.

---

# **Day 8: School Hut to Summit then down to Mweka Camp**

| Trekking time     | 12–15 hours |
| :---------------- | :---------- |
| **Climatic zone** | Arctic      |

Midnight summit push to Uhuru Peak followed by descent to Mweka Camp.

---

# **Day 9: Mweka Camp to Mweka Gate**

| Trekking time     | 3–4 hours  |
| :---------------- | :--------- |
| **Climatic zone** | Rainforest |

Final descent through forest to the gate.

---

## Notes:

* The Northern Circuit has the **highest summit success rate** due to longer acclimatization
* It is the **longest and least crowded route**
* Offers 360° views around the mountain
