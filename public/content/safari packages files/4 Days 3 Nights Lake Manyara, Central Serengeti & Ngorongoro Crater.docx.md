**4 Days/3 Nights Lake Manyara, Central Serengeti & Ngorongoro Crater**

**Day 1: Depart to Lake Manyara National Park for A Game Drive**

* **Depart to Lake Manyara:** Depart to Lake Manyara National Park (approx. 2 hours’ drive)  
* **Full Day Game Drive:** Explore large elephant herds, high concentration of baboons, diverse habitats including a soda lake, woodlands, which support a variety of wildlife, it also boasts a remarkable number of bird species including pink flamingos, attracted by the lake’s alkaline water.  
* **Accommodation:** Transfer to the lodge, overnight at **Octagon Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner

**Day 2: Depart to Central Serengeti for An Afternoon Game Drive**

* **Depart to Central Serengeti:** Depart to Serengeti National Park (approx. 4 – 5 hours’ drive)  
* **Afternoon Game Drive:** Explore the most popular region in the reserve for its abundant wildlife, large numbers of big cats and quintessential Serengeti landscapes of acacia-studded savanna. Resident wildlife makes this part of the Serengeti a fantastic year-round destination.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Serengeti KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: Full Day Game Drive at Serengeti National Park**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Depart for a full day game drive through this enormous beautiful park and explore its abundant wildlife.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Serengeti KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner


**Day 4: Depart to Ngorongoro Crater for A Game drive and Depart Back to Arusha**

* **Depart to Ngorongoro Crater:** Depart to Ngorongoro Crater (about an hour drive)  
* **Half Day Game Drive:** Explore a unique deep, volcanic crater, un-flooded and unbroken caldera, it’s about 200 kms across, 600 kms deep and 300 sq. kms in area. Approximately 30,000 animals live on the crater floor including the Big 5\!   
* **Depart to Arusha:** Later in the afternoon, depart back to Arusha (approx. 3 – 4 hours’ drive), arriving in the early evening.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
