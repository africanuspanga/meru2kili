**8 Days/7 Nights Lake Manyara National Park, Central and Northern Serengeti, Olduvai Gorge & Ngorongoro Crater**

**Day 1: Depart to Lake Manyara National Park for A Game Drive & Canopy Walkway Experience**

* **Depart to Lake Manyara:** Depart to Lake Manyara National Park (approx. 2 hours’ drive)  
* **Full Day Game Drive:** Explore large elephant herds, high concentration of baboons, diverse habitats including a soda lake, woodlands, which support a variety of wildlife, it also boasts a remarkable number of bird species including pink flamingos, attracted by the lake’s alkaline water.  
* **Treetop/Canopy Walkway**: The canopy leads you over stable, narrow bridges along the treetops through the beautiful untouched nature of the national park filled with monkeys, butterflies, and various exotic birds.  
* **Accommodation:** Transfer to the lodge, overnight at **Octagon Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner


**Day 2: Depart to Central Serengeti for An Afternoon Game Drive**

* **Depart to Central Serengeti:** Depart to Serengeti National Park (approx. 4 – 5 hours’ drive)  
* **Afternoon Game Drive:** Explore the most popular region in the reserve for its abundant wildlife, large numbers of big cats and quintessential Serengeti landscapes of acacia-studded savanna. Resident wildlife makes this part of the Serengeti a fantastic year-round destination.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Serengeti KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: Full Day Game Drive in Central Serengeti** 

* **Full Day Game Drive in Central Serengeti:** Depart for a full day game drive through this enormous beautiful park and explore its abundant wildlife.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Serengeti KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: Depart to Northern Serengeti for A Game Drive (Migration Experience)**

* **Depart to Northern Serengeti:** Depart to Northern Serengeti (approx. 3 \- 4 hours) with en-route game drive  
* **Game Drive in Northern Serengeti:** Follow the spectacular migration of wildebeests, zebra crossing the Mara River to Masai Mara.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Mara KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner 

**Day 5: Follow the Migration in Northern Serengeti** 

* **Full Day Game Drive in Northern Serengeti:** Explore the Northern Serengeti while experiencing this remarkable migration event until sunset.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Mara KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner 


**Day 6: Game Drive in Northern Serengeti and Depart Back to Central Serengeti**

* **Half Day Game Drive in Northern Serengeti:** Final experience of the migration in Northern Serengeti until noon.  
* **Depart to Central Serengeti:** Later, en-route game drive while departing to Central Serengeti arriving in the early evening.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Serengeti KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner 

**Day 7: Historical Visit to Olduvai Gorge and Depart to Ngorongoro Crater**

* **Depart to Olduvai Gorge Historical Site:** Depart to Olduvai Gorge (approx. 3 – 4 hours’ drive)  
* **Olduvai Gorge Site:**  a paleoanthropological site in located in Ngorongoro Conservation Area famous for its rich fossil discoveries and stone tools that have significantly advanced our understanding of early human evolution:  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at the crater rim, **Ngorongoro Kuhama Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner 

**Day 8: Descend to Ngorongoro Crater for A Game Drive and Depart Back to Arusha**

* **Game Drive at the Crater:** Explore a unique deep, volcanic crater, un-flooded and unbroken caldera, it’s about 200 kms across, 600 kms deep and 300 sq. kms in area. Approximately 30,000 animals live on the crater floor including the Big 5\!   
* **Depart to Arusha:** Later in the afternoon, depart back to Arusha (approx. 3 – 4 hours’ drive), arriving in the early evening.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
