  
**5 Days/4 Nights Tarangire NP, Southern & Central Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater**

**Day 1: Depart to Tarangire National Park for A Game Drive**

* **Depart to Tarangire:** Depart to Tarangire National Park (approx. 2 – 3 hours’ drive)  
* **Full Day Game Drive:** Encounter and explore high concentration of elephants, rich wildlife, diverse savanna landscape with massive baobab trees and impressive birdlife.  
* **Accommodation:** Transfer to the lodge, overnight at **Octagon Lodge**  
* **Meal Plan:** lunch and dinner

**Day 2: Depart to Southern Serengeti for An Afternoon Game Drive** 

* **Depart to Southern Serengeti:** Depart to Southern Serengeti (approx. 4 – 5 hours’ drive)  
* **Afternoon Game Drive:** In this time of year (December-March), the Great Migration moves to this southern area of Serengeti (Ndutu) coinciding with the calving season for wildebeests. It offers a unique game viewing experience with abundant wildlife including wildebeests, zebras and other herbivores as well as resident predators like lions and cheetahs.   
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Ndutu Migration Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 3: Full Day Game Drive in Southern Serengeti and Depart to Central Serengeti**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Depart for a full day game drive in this crucial part of the Serengeti in this time of the year until sunset.  
* **Depart to Central Serengeti:** Later, depart to Central Serengeti (approx. 2 hours’ drive)  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Serengeti KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner

**Day 4: Full Day Game Drive in Central Serengeti**

* **Full Day Game Drive:** Explore the most popular region in the reserve for its abundant wildlife, large numbers of big cats and quintessential Serengeti landscapes of acacia-studded savanna. Resident wildlife makes this part of the Serengeti a fantastic year-round destination.  
* **Accommodation:** Overnight at **Serengeti KatiKati Tented Camp**  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast, lunch and dinner


**Day 5: Depart to Ngorongoro Crater for A Game drive and Depart Back to Arusha**

* **Depart to Ngorongoro Crater:** Depart to Ngorongoro Crater (about an hour drive)  
* **Half Day Game Drive:** Explore a unique deep, volcanic crater, un-flooded and unbroken caldera, it’s about 200 kms across, 600 kms deep and 300 sq. kms in area. Approximately 30,000 animals live on the crater floor including the Big 5\!   
* **Depart to Arusha:** Later in the afternoon, depart back to Arusha (approx. 3 – 4 hours’ drive), arriving in the early evening.  
* **Meal Plan:** breakfast and lunch
